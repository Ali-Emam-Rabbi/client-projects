About Sastho seba

Built for clear, careful pharmacy shopping

Sastho seba is designed as a clean and trustworthy online pharmacy interface. The goal is to make medicine and healthcare product browsing simple, organized, and responsive across mobile and desktop.

This is a frontend-only project. Product data comes from a local JSON file, the cart is saved in browser localStorage, and login/signup forms use UI validation only.
